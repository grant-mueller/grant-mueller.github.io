Grant Mueller

Turning data chaos into clarity

I’m Grant Mueller—a data scientist turned data steward with a passion for creating systems that bring order, insight, and integrity to complex scientific data. I architect compliant, scalable data solutions, from Python scripts and cloud pipelines to interactive web apps, while leading teams through digital transformation in regulated environments.

With a background in antibody-drug conjugate (ADC) analytics and years of experience across statistical modeling, semantic data integration, and visualization, I specialize in making research and manufacturing data findable, accessible, interoperable, and reusable. Whether it’s developing interactive dashboards with Shiny, enriching biologics metadata via SPARQL, or translating stakeholder needs into AI-augmented workflows, I thrive at the intersection of science and strategy.

My mission is to turn data chaos into clarity—not just by crunching numbers, but by designing solutions that scale across teams, projects, and technologies. I believe good data stewardship starts with empathy: understanding how people use data, where bottlenecks live, and how smart tooling can simplify the path from insight to action.

When I’m not optimizing FAIR workflows or prototyping with Python, I’m probably testing a new AI tool, writing up project notes on GitHub, or sketching ideas for semantic-driven CMC processes.
